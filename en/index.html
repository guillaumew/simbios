Why?

There are several advantages with Green IT actions

For your organization

Meeting legal requirements

You or your clients are or will be affected by the REEN law or the CSRD.

Opening up to the market

Your clients may be sensitive to your environmental approach.

Employer branding

Companies with a sincere commitment are always more attractive.

For our planet

Fighting climate change

Digital technology contributes 3-4 % of global greenhouse gas emissions.

Preventing resource shortages

Rare metals are essential for the manufacturing of our digital equipment.

Preserving water and biodiversity

Beyond climate, the environmental impacts are numerous.

For humans

Against war

The extraction of certain metals contributes to fueling armed conflicts.

Limiting exploitation of people

Child labor, exposure to chemicals, overwork...

Access to water

Mineral extractions use large amounts of water, depriving local populations.
