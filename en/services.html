The usual Green IT cycle

In general, eco-design works in cycles of about one year.

After a phase of planning and preparing the funding, an audit of the service is initiated to define an action plan. These actions are monitored and supported by training the teams on the relevant topics.

The cycle concludes with the valorization of the approach (internally and externally).
